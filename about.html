Our Journey

Driven by passion long before
it became a business.

Our shared love for food, detail, and creativity brought the Brylo family together to build a place where everyone feels like part of a larger family. We believe food is more than a product—it is an experience, a memory, and a flavor that stays.


From day one, we committed to crafting everything with our own hands with honesty, high quality, and uncompromising craftsmanship. We promise to preserve what we started: fresh products, a warm atmosphere, and continuous development that reflects your expectations.

From us to you... The Brylo Family
In-House Craftsmanship

Founded in Al Ain

Brylo was founded in 2025 by a family of five individuals united by a shared passion for food. With expertise across coffee, baking, management, and finance, we built a brand that prioritizes authenticity and innovation.

Everything at Brylo is made 100% in-house, ensuring products that are fresh, unique, and consistently premium. From warm pastries to specialty beverages, Brylo aims to become the city's go-to destination for meaningful food experiences.
